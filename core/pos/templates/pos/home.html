About Uni360

Uni360 is a web-based campus navigation platform designed to help students, staff, and visitors easily locate specific rooms, buildings, or facilities within the university premises.

Developed by CIT-Innovation, Uni360 aims to make campus exploration seamless and stress-free through intuitive mapping, real-time route guidance, and location-based tools that enhance accessibility and convenience.

Whether you’re new to the campus or just trying to find your next class, Uni360 ensures you’ll never get lost again.
